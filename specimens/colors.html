Colors

PocketSeed lives on warm paper, anchored by deep ink. Brand teal/blue/green do the talking. Use one accent at a time and let the rest stay quiet.

Foundation

Ink, paper & line

Every surface in the system is one of these. --ps-paper is the canvas, --ps-ink is the dark counterpart, and the line tones are quiet enough to disappear at a glance.

Paper --ps-paper #f7f4ec
Paper warm --ps-paper-warm #efeadd
Card --ps-card #ffffff
Ink --ps-ink #1a2535
Ink soft --ps-ink-soft #2d3a4d
Line --ps-line #e5e0d3
Line soft --ps-line-soft #efeae0
Muted --ps-muted #6b7280
Brand

Teal, blue, purple, green

Pulled directly from the logo gradient. Teal is the default accent. Reserve purple for hero gradients (it appears in --ps-grad-orb); use green sparingly for "go / verified / impact" signals.

Teal --ps-teal #2dadc7
Blue --ps-blue #1f6f96
Purple --ps-purple #6a4ed8
Green --ps-green #4caf50
Green deep --ps-green-deep #2f7a3a
Leaf --ps-leaf #7ed957
